Common Questions

FAQ

Does it hurt?

Most clients describe it as a quick pinch. I use techniques to minimize discomfort, and the anticipation is usually worse than the piercing itself!

How long does healing take?

Lobes and septums typically heal in 6-8 weeks. Most other piercings can take 8-12 months. Healing varies by individual—I'll provide detailed aftercare instructions for your specific piercing.

What jewelry do you use?

I only use implant-grade titanium or 14k gold for initial piercings. These materials are hypoallergenic and safe for healing.

Can I bring my own jewelry?

For initial piercings, I recommend using jewelry I provide to ensure proper fit and quality. You can change to your own pieces once fully healed!
